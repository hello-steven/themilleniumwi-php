About Us

Pier 23 Cafe is a time honored San Francisco establishment located right on the SF bay. Family owned and operated since 1984, we have a full restaurant and bar with waterside dining. We are known for our fresh seafood, unique cocktails, and live music daily. If you want a true SF experience, this is the place to be.
